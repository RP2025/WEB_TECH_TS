About Shreya Ghoshal

Shreya Ghoshal is an Indian playback singer. Born on March 12, 1984, in Berhampore, West Bengal, she has become one of the most prominent and celebrated voices in the Indian music industry. Shreya's soulful renditions have left an indelible mark, earning her numerous awards and accolades. Shreya's journey in the world of music started at an early age, and over the years, she has lent her enchanting voice to a myriad of songs spanning various genres. Her ability to convey emotion through her singing has made her a favorite among music lovers worldwide. Apart from her playback singing career, Shreya has also ventured into television, serving as a judge on popular singing reality shows, showcasing her expertise and mentoring aspiring talents. Join us in celebrating the incredible journey of this musical sensation, Shreya Ghoshal!
